## Chinese Language Course Materials

- Course materials (PPTs/PDFs) are in Slovak (taught by Barbora Kamenská), but the students are Czech speakers
- When generating drills, translations, or pronunciation guides, use Czech — not Slovak
- PPTXs are stored in `resources/in_person_lessons/`
- Use `convert_pptx_to_md.py` to convert PowerPoint files into AI-friendly Markdown (output goes to `lessons_md/`)
- Use `generate_worksheets.py` to generate character writing worksheets + reference sheets (output goes to `worksheets/`)
